Boilerplate

Autism Advisor is an AI-powered iOS application that helps families understand and navigate complex special education documents such as Individualized Education Programs (IEPs). By combining generative AI with proprietary, state-specific legal knowledge graphs, Autism Advisor transforms dense educational and compliance language into clear, actionable, plain-language insights. The platform is designed to support families, advocates, and professionals in making more informed decisions—without replacing licensed educators, clinicians, or legal counsel.
